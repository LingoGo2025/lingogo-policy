LingoGo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 13, 2025

1. Introduction & Contact

Welcome to LingoGo (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at lingogo2025@outlook.com.

2. Information We Collect

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

• Provide, operate, and maintain the core features of the App (voice translation, image recognition).

• Improve and personalize your user experience.

• Store your conversations and analysis results locally on your device for your future reference.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your personal information. We only share your information in the following situations:

• Third-Party Service Providers: To provide translation and recognition features, your voice and image data are sent to the service provider you choose (Apple, Google Gemini, or OpenAI). We recommend you review their respective privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

• Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes.

All user-generated content (like history) is stored on your device, and we do not have access to it.

5. Data Storage & Security

All your personal data, including conversation history, image analysis records, and API keys, are securely stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not back up this data to any external servers. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no security system is impenetrable.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You can manage your data at any time through the App's settings. You can clear all history and settings. You can also revoke access to the microphone, camera, photo library, and location at any time through your iOS system settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

8. Policy Changes

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes within the App. It is recommended that you review this policy periodically for the latest information.

Addendum for Users in the European Union

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and related laws.

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate personal data.

Under certain conditions, you have the right to request that we delete your personal data.

Under certain conditions, you have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and to transmit it to another controller.

You have the right to object to our processing of your data based on legitimate interests.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the data protection supervisory authority in your country.

To exercise these rights, you can operate directly through the in-app features (such as deleting history, exporting data) or contact us via the email at the beginning of this policy.
